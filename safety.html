Life-Saving Rules

Stop Work Authority

Every person has the authority to stop unsafe work immediately.

PPE at All Times

Always wear appropriate PPE for the task and environment.

Permit to Work

Hot Work, Confined Space, and Electrical work require valid permits.

Lifting & Rigging Controls

All lifting operations must be planned and supervised by qualified personnel.

Working at Height

100% tie-off required when working above 2 meters.

Energy Isolation / LOTO

Always verify isolations before work begins.

Confined Space Entry

Entry only permitted with valid permit and continuous monitoring.

Pressure Testing Safety

Ensure all personnel are clear of test boundaries before pressurization.

Dropped Object Prevention

Secure all tools and materials to prevent falling objects.

Marine Deck Operations

Avoid pinch/crush hazards and stay out of line of fire during marine operations.
