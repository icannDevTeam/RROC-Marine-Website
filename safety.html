JSA / PTW Flow

Our structured 5-step process ensures every task is assessed, controlled, and communicated before work begins.

1. Identify Hazards

List all potential hazards associated with the task before any work begins.

2. Assess Risk

Evaluate risks and apply the ALARP principle — As Low As Reasonably Practicable.

3. Implement Controls

Put engineering, administrative, and PPE controls in place to mitigate risks.

4. Permit Issuance

Issue a valid Permit to Work with all controls verified and signed off.

5. Toolbox Talk

Brief all personnel on hazards, controls, and emergency procedures.
